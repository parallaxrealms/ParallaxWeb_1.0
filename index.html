Who am I?

My name is Christian, and I'm a freelance web developer and artist based out of Asheville, North Carolina. I have more than 8 years of experience as a developer. I use the latest technologies and frameworks to make sites that are responsive and visually-appealing. When I'm not making sites or designing apps, I'm working on art projects, games, or going on hikes and other meatspace activities. I'm available for work.
