Who am I?

My name is Christian, and I'm a freelance web developer and artist based out of Asheville, North Carolina. I have more than 8 years of experience as a web developer. Using the latest technologies and frameworks I try to make sites that are responsive and visually-appealing. When I'm not making or designing apps, I'm working on art projects, games, going on hikes or other meatspace activities. I am available for work. If you have a project in mind and need help executing it, contact me here
